# Stage 4 orientation-source matrix

Research snapshot: 2026-07-18. “Unknown” means the provider did not publish a value in the reviewed
official material. No row is an assertion of biological correctness.

## Support labels

- **Primary**: stable enough to design a deterministic adapter and reproducible fixture set.
- **Experimental**: opt-in, visibly labelled, and rejected unless coordinate identity is established.
- **Discovery only**: may identify external annotations but supplies no usable plane.
- **Deferred**: insufficient contract, incompatible geometry, or mismatched structure scope.

Provider URLs and primary references are collected in
[`stage4_research.md`](stage4_research.md). Rate limits that are not documented must be treated as
unknown; clients may not infer “unlimited”.
